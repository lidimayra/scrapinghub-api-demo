Most popular movies

lidimayra/scrapinghub-api-demo

A list of the most popular movies according to Rotten Tomatoes.

The starting point for building our list is https://www.rottentomatoes.com/browse/movies_in_theaters/sort:popular. That's where we get the titles from. Then, our spider accesses the pages associated with each one of the movies in that list to extract the movie's director name and synopsis.

The data was fetched using the Scrapy Cloud API.
